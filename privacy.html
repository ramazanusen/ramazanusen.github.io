Privacy Policy for Where 2 Watch

Last updated: December 4, 2024

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Where 2 Watch ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Don't Collect

Where 2 Watch is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app functions without requiring user accounts or personal data.

Data Usage

The app only accesses:

  • Movie information from TMDB (The Movie Database) API
  • Local storage for your watchlist (stored only on your device)

Third-Party Services

We use The Movie Database (TMDB) API to provide movie information. Please refer to TMDB's privacy policy for information about their data practices.

Local Storage

Your watchlist and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
